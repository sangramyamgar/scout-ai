# Company Overview and Information

## About the Company

Founded in 2016, the company is a leading fintech enterprise headquartered in Bangalore, India, with offices in Mumbai, Delhi, and Hyderabad. We specialize in providing innovative digital payment solutions, financial technology infrastructure, and enterprise software services to businesses across India and expanding into international markets.

## Mission, Vision, and Values

### Mission
To democratize access to financial technology by providing businesses of all sizes with secure, affordable, and easy-to-integrate payment and financial solutions.

### Vision
To become India's most trusted fintech partner, enabling 1 million businesses to participate in the digital economy by 2030.

### Core Values

**1. Customer First**
Every decision we make starts with understanding our customers' needs. We measure our success by the success of our customers.

**2. Innovation with Purpose**
We innovate not for the sake of innovation, but to solve real problems and create meaningful value. We embrace new technologies while ensuring stability and reliability.

**3. Integrity Always**
We maintain the highest standards of ethics and transparency. We handle financial data with utmost care and never compromise on security or compliance.

**4. Collaboration Over Competition**
We believe in building bridges, not walls. We partner with banks, fintechs, and businesses to create a stronger ecosystem for everyone.

**5. Continuous Learning**
The financial technology landscape evolves rapidly. We foster a culture of curiosity, learning, and adaptation.

## Products and Services

### Payment Solutions

**1. Payment Gateway**
Our flagship product enabling businesses to accept online payments through cards, UPI, net banking, and wallets.
- 99.99% uptime SLA
- 150+ payment methods supported
- Same-day settlement available
- PCI DSS Level 1 certified

**2. UPI Infrastructure**
Complete UPI stack for businesses including:
- UPI Collect for pull payments
- UPI Intent for app-based payments
- UPI QR for offline merchants
- UPI Mandate for recurring payments

**3. Payout Platform**
Automated bulk payment solution supporting:
- Bank transfers (NEFT, RTGS, IMPS)
- UPI payouts
- Card payments
- International remittances

### Enterprise Solutions

**1. Lending Infrastructure**
White-label lending platform with:
- KYC and verification APIs
- Credit scoring integration
- Loan management system
- EMI collection automation

**2. Banking-as-a-Service**
Enable businesses to offer banking features:
- Virtual accounts
- Prepaid card issuance
- Interest-bearing accounts
- Expense management
