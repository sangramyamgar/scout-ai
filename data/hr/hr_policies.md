### Performance Review Cycle

Performance reviews are conducted bi-annually in January and July. The review process includes:

1. **Self-Assessment**: Employees complete a detailed self-evaluation covering goals achieved, challenges faced, and development areas.

2. **Manager Assessment**: Direct managers provide feedback on performance, collaboration, and areas for growth.

3. **360-Degree Feedback**: For senior roles (Manager and above), feedback is collected from peers, direct reports, and cross-functional stakeholders.

4. **Calibration Sessions**: Department heads meet to ensure consistent rating standards across teams.

5. **Feedback Discussion**: One-on-one meetings between employee and manager to discuss ratings, feedback, and development plans.

### Resolution Process

1. **Filing**: Employee submits grievance through HRMS portal or directly to HR
2. **Acknowledgment**: HR acknowledges within 24 hours
3. **Investigation**: Fact-finding completed within 7 days
4. **Resolution**: Decision communicated within 14 days
5. **Appeal**: Employee may appeal to HR Director within 7 days
